Teriyaki Chicken

    BUY INGREDIENTS

  1. Buy chicken breast, brown sugar, green onions, bell peppers, salsa, Tsoyaki sauce, white onions, and one clove of garlic
  2. Prepare ingredients

    MARINATE INGREDIENTS
  3. Place ingredients in Ziploc bag, rotate gently until ingredients are randomized throughout bag
  4. Store in fridge for 3-4 days
  5. Remove chicken and ingredients from liquid in bag

    COOK CHICKEN
  6. Line bottom of Pyrex glass bowl withaluminumfoil and preheat oven to 425 degrees farenheight
  7. Scatter as evenly as possible chicken and ingredients from bag onto the aluminum foil
  8. After preheat is complete bake for 20-30 minutes or until internal temperature of chicken reaches 165*F
  9. Remove chicken from oven and let sit for 8-11 minutes

    SERVE CHICKEN
  10. Serve with fried rice or vegetables (salad is optional)

Creating a Dino Run game:
  1. Draw the game parallax background, ground, dinosaur.
  2. Get user input.
  3. Start the game and begin a timer.
  4. Change the horizontal speed of the floor and parallax background.
  5. Occasionally add in obstacles based on the value of the timer and check for user input.
  6. Keep repeating step 5 until player dies.
  7. Restart.
